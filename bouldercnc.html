Boulder CNC

Boulder CNC is atla's sister brand that takes on custom engineering/fabrication projects under the same roof. Rather than competing with the plethora of highly technical fabrication companies in the area, Boulder CNC is an approachable machine shop serving hobbyists and small businesses. People who want to build out a custom camper van, prototype their hobby furniture, or get a custom sign made for their storefront. I designed an inviting yet sleek brand identity and website to appease lay-folk that were builders in another life.
