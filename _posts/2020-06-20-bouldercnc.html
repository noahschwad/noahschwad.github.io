Boulder CNC

Boulder CNC is atla's sister brand that takes on custom engineering/fabrication projects under the same roof. Rather than competing with the plethora of highly technical fabrication companies in the area, Boulder CNC is an approachable machine shop serving hobbyists and small businesses. They are multi-disciplinary by nature, serving people working on projects ranging from custom furniture to specialized devices to custom storefront signs. I designed an inviting yet sleek brand identity and website to attract projects from machine nerds and lay-folk alike.
