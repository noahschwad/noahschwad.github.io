Colorado Carbon Fund

In 2017, I interned at an organization that had just acquired the Colorado Carbon Fund (CCF), a nonprofit dedicated to fighting climate change with in-state carbon offsets. The brand centers around a custom Colorado license plate that shows you offsetted your vehicle's carbon emissions, but the license plate and the brand itself were in need of a visual refresh. Rather than warning against a dystopian future, I emphasized the "think local, act global" aspect of the brand. I implemented a relaxing color palette and calm cloud imagery to reassure donors that doing their part will bring positive change.
