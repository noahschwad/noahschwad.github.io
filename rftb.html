Radio for the Blind

Radio for the Blind makes MusicalBooks—audiobooks decorated with music—for visually impaired and visually able children to enjoy together! To convey this idea, I made thorough use of kid-friendly illustrations to create a storybook aesthetic that also does a lot of the work of explaining what a MusicalBook is. And as always, accessibility was considered at every step, from braille on the promotional bookmark to optimizing the Bandcamp page for screen readers.
