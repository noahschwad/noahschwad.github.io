Radio for the Blind

Graphic Design, Branding, Illustration, Print Design, Art Direction

Radio for the Blind makes MusicalBooks—audiobooks decorated with music—for visually impaired and visually able children to enjoy together! To convey this idea, I made thorough use of kid-friendly illustrations to create a story-book aesthetic that also does a lot of the work of explaining what a MusicalBook is. And as always, accessibility was considered at every step, from braille on the promotional bookmark to optimizing the Bandcamp page for screen readers.
