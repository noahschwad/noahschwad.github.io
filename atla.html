Atla Design

Atla is a small, independent furniture company based in Boulder focusing on simplicity, sustainability, and durability. They came to me in need of a new website, more developed branding, marketing materials, and photo editing. My approach was to mirror the look of Atla's furniture with a modern, minimalist aesthetic that further emphasizes their three pillars.

Both this project and my case study are works in progress.
